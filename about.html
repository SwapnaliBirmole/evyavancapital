About Us

Our Philosophy

Evyavan is synonymous with “Lord Vishnu” who in Indian culture is believed to be the preserver and protector of the order of things, and maintains cosmic harmony in the universe.

These same attributes define our philosophy to be the preserver of our Investors’ capital – An ethos that is very much needed today in the financial sector. Building on this philosophy, we have imbibed in us the qualities of Trust, Security and Prosperity.

Founded by Raviprakash Bubna, a recognised Business Leader in Indian Credit space, Evyavan’s team comprises of the best and most passionate professionals from Financial Services and other sectors,

who bring with them rich experience and strong track records of investing and advisory, turnaround strategies, and strategic growth initiatives.

At Evyavan Capital, our business activities include a range of services across Credit Funds Management and Advisory Services. We believe in nurturing long-term relationships with both, our people and patrons, and this is the bedrock on which all of our business activities are based.

Our Work

A financial services group, Evyavan’s business activities include a suite of financial products ranging from Credit Funds to Advisory Solutions. Evyavan has been founded by experienced and motivated professionals with a strong track record in restructuring, turnaround, and growth strategies across businesses. Our diverse talent pool has hands-on expertise in Corporate Finance, Private Equity, Investment & Strategy, Financial & Legal Advisory, etc.

Evyavan’s range of innovative funds and advisory services leverage the on-ground management experience of the core team.

Our leadership has a combined experience of over 150 years (Domestic & Global markets). We prioritise relationships as they create long-term value for our investors and clients.
