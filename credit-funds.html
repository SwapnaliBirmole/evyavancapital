Credit Funds

Overview

Evyavan Capital is a credit platform that is sponsoring multiple Credit Funds under the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Alternative Investment Funds) Regulations, 2012. These Credit Funds are focused on generating superior risk adjusted returns by investing in structured debt opportunities emerging across sectors. Evyavan Assets Management Limited will be the Investment Advisor to these funds.

While the India Growth story continues to remain intact, a large disruption is visible in the existing credit landscape as businesses continue to demand customized credit solutions to meet their unique financing requirements.

Traditional lenders such as Banks, Non-Banking Finance Companies and Mutual Funds are struggling to keep up with this demand thereby creating a huge vacuum for credit supply in a large and attractive market.

Credit Funds sponsored by Evyavan Capital aim to bridge this gap by providing structured credit financing to high-quality businesses and entrepreneurs.

THE EVYAVAN EDGE

The Founder and Team Evyavan have a combined experience of over 150 years in the Structured Credit space and have successfully built and managed credit books of over US$ 6 bn. They have successfully executed credit and financing strategies to maximize risk-adjusted returns and are committed towards this objective.

Leveraging on Team Evyavan’s past experience in maximizing risk-adjusted returns, a structured and rigorous approach has been adopted:

Robust governance practices backed by an independent and empowered Board, and experienced Investment Committee members

Proprietary and unique deal origination through unparalleled industry relationships of the Founder and the senior team – Long-term relationships are nurtured by continuously engaging with clients and providing relevant solutions at all stages of an organization’s lifecycle

Stringent multi-stage deal evaluation process to identify attractive deals

Creating customised credit solutions for shortlisted deals basis the team’s extensive experience and in-depth understanding of the clients’ business

Investment Focus

Evyavan invests in the following types of investment opportunities:

Funding to boost the growth of high potential businesses or capacity expansions

Interim financing for companies targeting inorganic growth opportunities and corporate events

Funding solutions for companies battling operating problems or financing difficulties
