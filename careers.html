Careers

Life At Evyavan

Evyavan is home to a team of very experienced individuals with a wide range of skillsets – The team comprises of deal makers, fund managers, credit & research analysts, operation ninjas, and seasoned professionals who have built large businesses.

Becoming a part of Evyavan gives you a unique opportunity of building your skillsets and fast-tracking your career in a highly supportive work culture that thrives on initiative and teamwork. Our team members face some of the toughest challenges in Indian Financial Services and come up with customised, structured solutions to each of the unique issues confronting our clients.

Evyavan is the ideal workplace for both experienced professionals as well as fresh graduates who are looking to enhance their skills, leap into an extremely rewarding career trajectory, and for the opportunity to work alongside a team that takes on some of the most interesting challenges in Indian Financial Services.
