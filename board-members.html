Raviprakash Bubna

Founder & CEO

Raviprakash Bubna has over 3 decades of experience in Corporate Finance, Structured & Real Estate Finance, Quantitative Financing, Project Financing, Debt Advisory, Risk Management and Distressed Solutions.

He has served in leadership roles at Edelweiss Group and Aditya Birla Group and has set several benchmarks in the industry through a focused solution-based approach to clients, robust underwriting practices and a quick turnaround to clients’ financing needs.

In his last role at Edelweiss Group, Ravi was the Managing Director & CEO of ECL Finance Limited and had set strategic direction for Edelweiss Group’s Financing business, building a robust credit platform and scaling wholesale financing book to over US $4 billion (end Mar-2019) – His skilled leadership led to the financing business becoming the largest contributor to Edelweiss Group’s revenue mix.

Ravi also served as Board Member of several Edelweiss Group Companies, was part of multiple Investment and Credit Committees; and was a key member of the Corporate Risk Committee at the Edelweiss Group level.

Previously, Ravi spent more than a decade at the financial services arm of Aditya Birla Group where his last assignment was as Joint President & Country Head at Birla Global Finance.

Ravi holds a Master’s degree in Business Administration and Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from Mumbai University.

He is also known for his strong social network and philanthropic interventions through his senior roles at Rotary club & ISKCON, and has raised > USD 20 mn for various charitable causes.

Establishing trust, helping companies grow under strategic guidance & transparency is integral to Ravi’s approach. Evyavan Capital has been founded by him on this key tenet of building long-term and enduring institutional relationships.

Pankaj Bagla

Executive Director

Pankaj is a qualified Chartered Accountant with 19 years of experience in Financial Services encompassing Investments, Asset & Fund Management, Financial Due Diligence, and Statutory Audit. He also holds a Bachelors of Commerce from Calcutta University.

In his previous role, Pankaj was Vice President - Fund Management at ECL Finance Limited and was responsible for deploying & monitoring funds, and managing the deal cycle – deal sourcing, financial modeling, due diligence, negotiations, closure & monitoring the portfolio companies. From 2017 to 2018, he handled multiple proposals accruing to ~$120 mn in deal value.

Pankaj's extensive career in Investments and Fund Management extends further to 2009 - when he was a Manager at IEC Operations Management, an internationally-based real estate fund, after which he took up the position of General Manager of Real Estate Investments at the Mirah Group ($250 interests in real estate, hotels, and hospitality) from 2011 to 2014. Between 2014 and 2017, he was the Vice President of Investments at Arthaveda Fund Management, a private equity arm of Dewan Housing Finance Corporation Ltd.

After clearing his Chartered Accountancy professional examinations, between 2002 and 2009, Pankaj worked with SPS Metals & Alloys, then conducted Statutory Audit as an Assistant Manager with Ernst & Young, and worked in the Financial Due Diligence practice of KPMG.
