Advisory Services

Our Advisory Services are tailored to the unique dynamics of Indian markets. We assist clients by assessing their capital needs, structuring, due diligence, deal closing, and future exits.

We advise and aid Indian companies to raise capital (debt as well as equity, in various forms & structures) from domestic and international markets. We also assist companies looking to expand their reach in the India by identifying the right partners, either through joint ventures, acquisitions (Domestic / In-bound M&A), or investments. We work with global and Indian companies by restructuring, financing and asset sale alternatives, and accounting for economic impacts and risks.

We provide customised transaction advice to financial and strategic investors interested in special situations. These include transaction structuring, valuation, asset / strategic business unit monetisation, and court-driven reorganisations.

Apart from traditional institutional funding, we work with private Indian organisations to assist them in developing an in-depth capital markets strategy (Capital Market Advisory), and position them for long term financial sustainability, regardless of whether they choose to pursue a strategic transaction or an Initial Public Offering (IPO-Advisory).

Advisory Solutions

Evyavan is well-equipped with innovative and customised solutions to handle situations where a client realigns and renegotiates the capital structure.

Being a partner through the entire lifecycle of the corporate, we address all of their financial and strategic growth needs encompassing the following advisory services:

Strategic Fund Raising

PE, VC, ECBs, FCCBs and Other Foreign Currency Loans

Debt Syndication

Structured & Mezzanine Finance, Project Finance, Acquisition Finance, Syndication Of Term Loans & Structured Debt Obligations and Other Forms of Funding

Distressed Assets Advisory

Turnaround Strategies, Debt Restructuring, NCLT Support and ARC Assistance & Funding

Transaction Advisory

Asset Sale, Takeover / Buyout and M&A

How We Work?

Our Advisory team consists of experienced individuals who are experts in debt advisory and various forms of capital raising. Long-standing relationships with corporates, financial sponsors and debt providers enables Evyavan to provide a complete range of advisory services from Origination to Placement.

Our approach involves spending time with clients to understand their unique requirements and objectives, designing and structuring financial instruments most suited to address their financial needs, and shortlisting institutions best placed to provide these funding solutions. We also assist our clients during the negotiation process thereby providing a complete range of advisory solutions across our clients’ need spectrum.

Evyavan’s expertise lies in recognising emerging industry themes and positioning transactions within their context. Our team assists in getting the most favourable terms for debt financing by employing a comprehensive solicitation process. The objective is to pick the right lenders to partner our clients in executing their business strategies.

Evyavan’s team ensures that every client’s financing needs are matched to the right financing partner at competitive rates. Our network of investors across the globe, including Indian and foreign banks, multilateral institutions, private equity and hedge funds, special situation funds, NBFCs, insurance companies and mutual funds, is what helps Evyavan stand out in providing the best possible and fully compliant financial solutions.
